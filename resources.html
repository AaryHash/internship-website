My Resources

Resource 1: Hard Fork. While I do not find this podcast to be completely unbiased, I feel that it has an interesting view and up to date news on technology--especially artificial intelligence--which is important for me to keep up with consistently as an ITI professional.

Roose, K., & Newton C. (Hosts). (2020-present). Hard Fork. [Audio podcast]. NYT. https://www.nytimes.com/column/hard-fork

Resource 2: Upwork. This website is something I learned about during my 2025 fall internship. While it is useful for making money and getting jobs, I find it far more useful for gaining experience with practical, hands-on work. It takes time to get clients, but when you do, the work you do is priceless for your portfolio and technological understanding.

Upwork. (2015-2025). https://www.upwork.com/

Resource 3: Northeast Big Data Innovation Hub. This was a resource I learned about during the 2025 SC&I Mingle networking event. This is a community that young data scientists can use to engage in projects with like-minded individuals and bolster their field knowledge.

Northeast Big Data Innovation Hub. (2015-2025). https://nebigdatahub.org/
